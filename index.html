My name is Anuj, and I live in New York City. I'm a co-founder and CTO of Vesper, an AngelPad #9 startup focused on helping businesses better manage their offices using technology. Previously, I was a Project Manager at Tinypass (now called Piano), a TriBeCa-based SaaS platform that helps publishers monetize their digital content.

I graduated from Columbia University in 2011 with a degree in Economics-Political Science. After graduation, and before I entered the tech world, I worked in finance, first as an investment banking analyst at UBS, and then as a high-yield credit investment analyst at a NY-based hedge fund.

My passion is to build smart products that solve everyday problems. I'm on AngelList, GitHub, LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, and you can email me here.
